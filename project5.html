Exploring California's Agriculture in Stardew Valley

Overview

In my Tableau dashboard, "Stardew Valley x California," I creatively merged the charming aesthetics of the beloved game Stardew Valley with real-world USDA agricultural data from California. This interactive visualization offers insights into the state's crop production, farm economics, and regional agricultural trends. By blending nostalgic game elements with factual data, the dashboard aims to make agricultural statistics more engaging and accessible to a wider audience.
